# Engineer retro template (SC-004)

Submit one response per engineer (anonymous or named):

1. Relative to baseline CI/deploy wait time for your recent PRs, did this feel **faster**, **about the same**, or **slower**?
2. If you answered **slower** or **about the same**, which job or deployment phase dominated your wait?
3. One concrete follow-up that would most improve confidence or speed for your workflow.
